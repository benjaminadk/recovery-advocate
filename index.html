  About Us

  Our Mission

  •   Advocate for all our clients to the fullest extent of our abilities
  •  Find appropriate and suitable treatment options for all our clients
  •  To make sure our client's attend clean, safe, and helpful facilities
  •  To NEVER give up on anybody
More people, everyday, need treatment as the opioid epidemic rips apart our country. Those struggling with addiction are vulnerable and the advocacy of these people becomes imperative. My brother was vulnerable when he passed away in a medical facility because he did not receive adequate care. Out of the trauma of loosing him came my passion. I made it my business to do everything I can in order to prevent this from happening to others. Northeastern Recovery Advocates becomes your voice when you are unsure of what you want to say. We travel your journey of treatment with you, encouraging you every step of the way and we make sure you are taken care of so you can thrive in your treatment. We have connections for treatment centers across the country and our only aim is for you to enter one that is beneficial for your recovery. There are so many corrupt facilities out there. We have taken the honor of weeding through them to allow clear options from ethical resources.

 How to Use This Site

 Option 1

  1. Fill out the form at the bottom of this page
  2. We will be in touch soon after you submit
  3. Based on your information we will find the best treatment option for you
  4. We will make the necessary calls and schedule your treatment
  5. We will put you in touch with your treatment facility
  6. You will be off on the beautiful and exciting journey of recovery

 Option 2

  1. Use the interactive map provided by SAMSHA
  2. Just follow the instructions we have provided for you
  3. Fill out the form but check the option "No" for the question "Do you want us to help find you a treatment center"
  4. Still complete the form in case you have questions about the proecess
  5. We have a lot of information about treatment and the process of planning a stay at a treatment facility. We can help.
  6. Set up your own treatment and keep us posted on your progress, if you want.

 Frequently Asked Questions

  •   How much does your service cost?
    •  Nothing. All of our services are free.

  •   How much is treatment going to cost?
    •  That depends. Your insurance, which facility you pick, your length of stay, and other factors determine the cost of treatment. We will help set you up in the most cost effective way possible for your desired treatment.

  •   How long will it take to get into treatment?
    •  It varies. We try to get you in as soon as possible. It could be the same day for something like detox OR it might take 1-2 weeks(or more) to find an open bed in an inpatient facility. This is why we are so valueable. We have relationships with providers and don't stop calling until we find a bed for our clients.

  •   Will my information be kept private?
    •  Yes. Keeping your information private is a top priority. Substance abuse can be a delicate topic so we follow strict legal guidelines.Our privacy policy discusses our guidelines. We ONLY use your information to help you find the the most appropriate treatment.

 Interactive Treatment Locator

  How to Use This Map

  1. Enter your zip code or city/state in upper right corner
  2. Click on your city from the dropdown. The map will update.
  3. Where is says "Service" check the box "Substance Abuse"
  4. You can narrow your search by "type", "setting", "payment" and more
  5. You will see the map updating as you do this
  6. There will be contact info generated as you narrow your search
  7. If you have trouble using the map contact us and we will help
  8. There are also some links in the map for tutorials and help
  9. Let us know what you find and we will set everything up for you
